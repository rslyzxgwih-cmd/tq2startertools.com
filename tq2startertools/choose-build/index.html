Recommendation

For the Static MVP, start with melee or balanced caster.

Why: these paths are easiest to explain without finalized game data and give new players clear next decisions around mastery, skill priority, and beginner build type.

Best for: Beginner and relaxed players. Endgame and SSF recommendations remain placeholders until systems are stable.
